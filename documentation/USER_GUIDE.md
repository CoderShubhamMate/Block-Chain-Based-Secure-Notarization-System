# USER GUIDE
## BBSNS: Public Notarization Services

### 1. Account Enrollment
1.  **Registration**: Visit the BBSNS Web Application and navigate to **Sign Up**.
2.  **3-Factor Setup**:
    - Provide a secure password.
    - Connect your **MetaMask** wallet and sign the registration message.
    - Perform the **Liveness Check** via your camera for biometric enrollment.
3.  **Verification**: Your account will be marked as "Pending" until initial KYC liveness is verified by the system.

### 2. Document Submission
1.  **Upload**: Authenticate and select **Upload Document**.
2.  **Hashing**: The system automatically generates a unique SHA-256 fingerprint for your file.
3.  **Selection**: Choose a verified **Notary** from the public list to review your submission.

### 3. Tracking & History
- Track the status of your documents (Pending/Verified/Rejected) in your **User Dashboard**.
- Once verified, you can download a **Notarization Certificate** linked to the on-chain transaction hash.
